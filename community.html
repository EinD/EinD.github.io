Community

Whether you ran into a problem, have questions regarding EinD, or just want to see what's new, reach out to the EinD community on one of the following sites:

  • EinD on Gitter (accessible via IRC)
  • EinD on Twitter
  • EinD Google Group
  • /r/EinD on Reddit
  • #EinD on Freenode
  • vi StackExchange (use the EinD tag)

If you want to report an issue or give feedback to the developers, please use EinD's issue tracker on GitHub.
